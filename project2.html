Deep Q-Learning for Lunar Landing

Developing neural networks entirely from scratch to facilitate the training of an agent across a diverse array of more than 200 unique scenarios. Employing sophisticated Deep Q-Learning methodologies, which encompass leveraging the renowned Belleme Equation, renowned for its efficacy in tackling complex problems. Additionally, integrating Experience Replay into the framework to expedite the agent's learning process and enhance its capacity for understanding nuanced environments. Furthermore, utilizing Gymnasium Libraries to visualize and interpret the outcomes, thereby enabling comprehensive analysis and optimization of the agent's performance.
